Nature of serviceAuricular acupuncture and ear seeds are complementary wellness services involving placement of small needles or adhesive seeds on specific points of the outer ear. This is a wellness service and does not constitute a medical diagnosis, treatment, or clinical therapeutic relationship.
Risks & side effectsPossible side effects include temporary soreness, bruising, bleeding, or skin irritation; lightheadedness or fainting; infection (rare, minimized with sterile technique); and allergic reaction to adhesive or metal materials.
Ear seedsIf applied, ear seeds may remain 3–5 days. Remove if excessive discomfort, redness, or swelling occurs. Avoid prolonged submersion. Seeds may fall off and should be discarded safely.
Voluntary participationParticipation is entirely voluntary. I may withdraw consent or ask the practitioner to stop at any time, without penalty.
Not a substitute for medical careThis service does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
